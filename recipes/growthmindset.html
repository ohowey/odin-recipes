Growth Mindset

Description

Growth mindset is the understanding that sometimes difficult subjects take a long time to understand but that it is possible. This is opposed to a fixed mindset which believes that anything that doesn't come immediately will never come. If you have a growth mindset you will put in the effort to understand something better. If you want to accomplish a project it doesn't matter how long it takes as long as you do it. It's useful to go in knowing that it will be hard, not beating yourself up when it is hard, and celebrating your success despite the difficulty.

Ingredients

  • Grit
  • Persistence
  • Learning
  • Struggling

Steps

  1. Find a difficult project that you hope to accomplish. Something that won't be easy but is possible.
  2. Struggle with it but keep in mind that you can do it!
  3. Persist through all the obstacles with grit and determination.
  4. Celebrate your success!
