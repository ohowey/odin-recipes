Getting Unstuck

Description

When attempting something like the Odin Project it isn't a question of if but of when you'll get stuck. Therefore it's important to be prepared with strategies to become unstuck. Being stuck can kill your motivation so it's best to extricate yourself ASAP. And always remember, being stuck is part of the process.

Ingredients

  • Google
  • Taking a break
  • Discord
  • Review

Steps

  1. First try googling the answer. You aren't the first person to have had this problem.
  2. Maybe your problem is an insufficient grasp of material that came before. If this is the case then review past lessons.
  3. If neither of those work then try taking a break. Our minds are constantly making new connections in what is called diffuse learning.
  4. If you've put in a solid effort and are still stuck then ask someone for help. This could be someone you know or through TOP discord.
